Why Choose Clear Aligners?

Clear aligners are one of the most popular orthodontic treatments today, especially among teens and adults who prefer a subtle way to improve their smiles. Unlike traditional braces, clear aligners are transparent and removable. This means you can eat, drink, brush, and floss normally without worrying about wires or brackets.

They work by applying gentle pressure to your teeth, gradually shifting them into their correct positions over time. Each set of aligners is custom-made for your mouth, ensuring a precise and comfortable fit throughout the treatment process.

How Clear Aligners Work

At Sabinsky Orthodontics, DDS, your journey to a straighter smile begins with a detailed consultation and digital scan of your teeth. These scans are used to create a personalized treatment plan that outlines every step of your smile transformation.

You’ll receive a series of custom-made aligners that you’ll wear for about 20–22 hours a day. Every one to two weeks, you’ll switch to a new set that gradually moves your teeth closer to their ideal positions. The entire process typically takes between 6 to 18 months, depending on your individual case.
